About

Sri Uttaradi Math, established by Sri Madhvacharya, traces its origin from the Supreme Lord, Sri Hamsanamaka Paramatma. A galaxy of saints and sages have adorned this peeta and all the peetadipatis of Sri Uttaradi Math are known for their vast learning and erudition in the sastras, their tapascharya, and their firm faith in Sri Madhvacharya’s tattvavada.

One of the pontiffs of Sri Uttaradi Math is Sri Satyadharma tirtha. He ruled the Vedanta samrajya from 1743. to 1830. He was a great scholar, well-learned in the sastras, and an extempore poet. All his works reveal his unique style, his literary, poetic skills, and his mastery of Sanskrit. His intense devotion and tapascharya earned him the glorious vision of Gangamata. Sri Satyadharma tirtha hailed from a family called Navaratna. True to his lineage, after his sanyasashrama he is a shining gem, a navaratna, in the crown of Sri Uttaradi Math. His moola Brindavan is situated on the bank of river Ghataprabha in Kalloli, the stream of gold, near Jamakhandi. The moola Brindavan of his disciple Sri Narasimha Wodeyar is also situated in Kalloli.

Sri (1008) Sri Satyapramoda tirtha conducted his Utsava in Kalloli in the year 1988. Now, in the year 2023, Sri (1008) Sri Satyatma tirtha will conduct his 28th Utsava in this sacred place. In this year, we are blessed with Adikamasa during the month of Shravana. The year 2023 will also be the 193rd year of Sri (1008) Sri Satyadharma tirtha’s Aradhana. Sri (1008) Sri Satyatma tirtha will conduct the bicentenary Aradhana celebrations of Sri 1008 Sri Satyadharma tirtha during his Utsava.

So come one! come all!! Come to receive the shower of blessings from Sri (1008) Sri Satyadharma thirtha and Sri (1008) Sri Satyatma tirtha. Come to Kalloli to serve our revered guru Sri (1008) Sri Satyatma tirtha. Come to Kalloli and overcome all obstacles and problems in life. Come to listen to Sri (1008) Sri Satyatma tirtha’s soul-stirring and soul-awakening golden words that are far more precious than all the gold and treasures of the world.

How To Reach

Kalloli is located 11 kms. from Jamakhandi, Bagalkot District, Karnataka

By Bus

Kalloli has frequent busses (every 15 mins) from Jamakhandi (~11kms) from 6am to 8.15pm. For more details Click here

By Train

Jamakhandi is the nearest railway station which has good connectivity with Bangalore with daily 4 trains connecting the two cities

By Flight

Jamakhandi is the nearest airport (~24kms). Kalloli can be reached from airport via taxi
